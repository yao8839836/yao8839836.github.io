General

I am currently a senior researcher at Tencent. Before that, I was a Postdoc researcher at Northwestern University, working with Prof. Yuan Luo. In 2017, I got my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University. Before that, I received my B.E.degree from Sichuan University in 2012. My research interest is primarily in Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Data Mining. You can find me at dryao [AT] tencent.com or learn more about me with my [Google Scholar], [Github], and CV.

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Data Mining, Medical Informatics.
