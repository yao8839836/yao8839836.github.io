General

I am currently an Associate Professor at Sun Yat-sen University. From 2019 to 2024, I was a senior researcher at Tencent. From 2018 to 2019, I was a Postdoc researcher at Northwestern University, working with Prof. Yuan Luo. In 2017, I got my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University. I received my B.E.degree from Sichuan University in 2012. My research interest is primarily in Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Data Mining. You can find me at yaoliang3 [at] mail.sysu.edu.cn or learn more about me with my [Google Scholar], [Github], and [CV].

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Data Mining, Medical Informatics.
