About

I am currently an Associate Professor at Sun Yat-sen University. From 2019 to 2024, I was a senior researcher at Tencent. From 2018 to 2019, I was a Postdoc researcher at Northwestern University, working with Prof. Yuan Luo. In 2017, I got my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Baogang Wei and Prof. Yin Zhang. I received my B.E. degree from Sichuan University in 2012. My research interest is primarily in Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Data Mining.
